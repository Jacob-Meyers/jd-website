Echo35

PC Video-Game - Created By Jeyers Development

Echo35 is a PvPvE Online Multiplayer FPS set in a near future where there is a threat on the battlefield. Your mission is to eliminate the opposing forces on their vast home turf or in quickly performed close-quarters combat.

JOIN THE DISCORD

Echo35 is an Early Access Game

Why Early Access?

“As an Indie game developer it is hard to make a game fully complete on release without having time to improve and change the gameplay. It will also help get the game more attention to help provide suggestions. The main reason of Echo35 being in early access is that there will be a lot more room for improvement, helping find gameplay balance, find bugs, and tune mechanics.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Early access is expected to last approximately 2-3 years, this depends on development progress, community feedback, and feature completion. The Early Access period are subject to change.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“I plan to achieve a polished Open-world gamemode along with balanced NPC AI, to provide a immersive experience with meaningful gameplay. In addition, I intent to significantly expand on the weapon system by introducing a wide variety of guns, each being highly customizable to your liking and play-style.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The core close-quarters combat modes (1v1, 2v2, 3v3, and 5v5) are currently implemented and will be continuously refined and expanded on over time. However, the open-world mode is still in early development and will require significantly more work and time, so it may not be fully stable or feature-complete until release.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“No, the game will always be free with NO Pay-To-Win components included. Priced DLC will be purely cosmetic.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I will involve the community by listening to their suggestions but still leaving the "frame" of the game the same. You can add suggestions through the Steam Forums, on Discord, or on the Jeyers Development Website.”

About This Game

TEAM BASED OPEN-WORLD BATTLES

Play as one large team and eliminate the opposing forces to cash up and unlock new weapons and attachments. Start in the head quarters building, then fly out in vehicles with your buddies. Stealthily kill the enemies or be bold and go in guns blazing. But make sure to grab all the intel you can to gather information on the next plans to clear that area of any new enemy troops, securing it. Establish dominance over every town, location, and city. Only then you are ready to arm up and clear their most protected facility. In there, who knows how much lead there is to be fired, how many explosives to be detonated, and how many secrets to be found?

CLOSE-QUARTERS COMBAT

Eliminate other players in tighter locations with your team or on some one-versus-one action. This comes as a quick burst of thrilling gameplay and plenty of replay-ability as several maps are available that range in size, theme, and strategy. Team types include: 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, 5v5.

Mature Content Description

TEAM BASED OPEN-WORLD BATTLES

There is violence in Echo35 as there is actions of firing weapons upon other players in-game. Violent references and actions involving: firearms, knives, long-bladed weapons, references to illegal drugs (found settings, not used), cartoonish blood and gore effects.
